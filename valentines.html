Loved Days

Overview

Valentine’s Day, celebrated on February 14th, is a special occasion dedicated to expressing love and affection between intimate partners, friends, and family. It originated from the ancient Roman festival honoring Saint Valentine, a symbol of love and compassion. Today, people celebrate by exchanging cards, flowers, chocolates, and heartfelt messages to show appreciation and deepen their bonds. Valentine’s Day is a celebration of romance, kindness, and the joy of meaningful connections.
